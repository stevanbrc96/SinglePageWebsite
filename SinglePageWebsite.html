Mojito

A classic, refreshing Cuban highball.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz White rum
  • 1 oz Fresh lime juice
  • 2 tsp Sugar
  • 6-8 Fresh mint leaves
  • Soda water
  • Lime slices and mint sprig for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Gently muddle mint leaves with lime juice and sugar in a sturdy glass.
  2. Add rum and stir lightly.
  3. Fill the glass with crushed ice.
  4. Top with soda water.
  5. Garnish with a lime slice and a mint sprig.

Old Fashioned

A timeless cocktail, often considered the first cocktail.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Bourbon or rye whiskey
  • 1 Sugar cube (or 1 tsp sugar)
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • A few drops of plain water
  • Orange peel for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Place sugar cube in an Old Fashioned glass.
  2. Add bitters and a few drops of water. Muddle until sugar is dissolved.
  3. Fill the glass with large ice cubes.
  4. Pour in whiskey and stir until well-chilled.
  5. Garnish with a large orange peel, twisted over the drink.

Margarita

A sour and tangy Mexican cocktail.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Tequila (Blanco or Reposado)
  • 1 oz Fresh lime juice
  • 0.75 oz Triple sec (Cointreau or Grand Marnier recommended)
  • Salt for rim (optional)
  • Lime wedge for garnish

Instructions:

  1. If desired, rim a chilled glass with salt by rubbing a lime wedge around the rim and dipping it in salt.
  2. Combine tequila, lime juice, and triple sec in a shaker with ice.
  3. Shake well until thoroughly chilled.
  4. Strain into the prepared glass filled with fresh ice.
  5. Garnish with a lime wedge.
